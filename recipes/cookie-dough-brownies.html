Cookie Dough Brownies

Description

These rich, delicious brownies are the hit of any party! Fudgy brownie base with a rich chocolate chip cookie dough layer on top, you will not be able to have just one!

Ingredients

Brownie Layer

  • 3/4 cup (1.5 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1.5 cups of bittersweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all purpose flour

Cookie Dough Layer

  • 3/4 cup (1.5 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature or almost melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbls milk or cream (or Bailey's)
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup miniature chocolate chips
