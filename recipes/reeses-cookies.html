Reese's Cookies

Description

Delectable, delicious, chocolate, peanut butter cookies are so soft and chewy. These will statify anyone's craving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter room temperature
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup Reese's Pieces

Directions

  1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees F and line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth
  3. Add vanilla and eggs and combine
  4. Add flour, baking soda, cocoa and salt and beat until dough forms
  5. Stir in chocolate chips
  6. Roll into 1" balls and place 2" apart on baking sheet. Bake for 8-9 mins
