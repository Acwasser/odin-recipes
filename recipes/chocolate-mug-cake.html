Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

Need a quick sweet treat? This is for you! Quick and easy, This single serving cake is the best quick fix!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp Dutch or regular cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2-3 tsp oil
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in to microwave safe mug and mix well
  2. Add liquid ingredients and stir well
  3. Microwave for 40-60 seconds
  4. Wait to cool then enjoy
